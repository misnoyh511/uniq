Frequently Asked Questions

How Does Proof Count Unique Visitors?

A unique visitor is anyone that visits one of your pages with our pixel loaded on the page. A visitor may have multiple pageviews and sessions, but they will still only be tracked as a single visitor if they do not clear their browser history or switch to a new device.

Will Proof Work With My Website?

Yes, we probably will work with your website. Proof integrates with most website platforms out there and we are adding new integrations every day. You can see some of the integrations we already have listed on our features page.

Does Proof Have An Affiliate Program?

If you have an audience you think would enjoy using Proof, message us in the live chat and we'll get you connected to the right person.

Does Proof Just Work For Purchases?

Proof works for purchases, lead captures, email opt-ins, appointment bookings, and more. Basically, we can create notifications for anywhere on your website that a customer fills out a form.

Can I Create Fake Notifications?

Heck no. We built Proof because we were tired of all the dishonest marketing out there on the internet. We were tired of being scammed and trying to figure out if someone was legit or not. We are dedicated to making every notification 100% legit.

What Happens If I Exceed The Limit For My Plan?

Don't worry, we will not drop or throttle your data! Once you exceed your plan's monthly limit, you'll receive multiple notification emails before you hit your limit. When you go 110% over your limit we will automatically upgrade your account to the next plan.

Am I Locked In To A Long-Term Contract?

No, your Proof account is month to month and you can cancel anytime.

Where Does Proof Get My Customer's Profile Pictures From?

We search the internet for publicly available social profile images that your customers have attached to their email address. These are posted online with consent, and your customers can opt out of having their information shared on Proof by reaching out to us, so you're covered.

Can I Upload Past Customer Purchases?

Right now, no. We know some folks are legit, but we also know if this was an option, people would abuse it and upload fake lists to make themselves look better. Not cool.
